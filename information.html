About Vision'97

Vision'97 is a lifestyle brand focused on creating apparel that represents our vision for the future. Our products are designed in Ghana with attention to quality and detail.

Our mission is to create clothing that connects people and shares our positive outlook through distinctive designs and quality garments.

Return Policy

All sales are final.

No refunds or exchanges.

Shipping & Processing

We ship worldwide.

Please allow 7-10 business days to process orders.

Please allow 10-15 days to arrive once shipped.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email.

We are not responsible for any lost or stolen packages.
